Risk: filters based on measured audio traits should stay labeled as coarse triage. They can narrow review work, but they cannot prove intent, source identity, or meaning without human review or additional analysis. Detector matches are model output, not findings: an unreviewed match proves nothing, and even a reviewer-confirmed match records a human judgment about similarity, not the cause or source of the sound. Reference versions matter - a match made by v1 is not re-validated when the reference is tuned to v2.
